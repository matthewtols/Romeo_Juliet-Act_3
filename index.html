Act 3

Scene 1

at the beginning of this scene, Mercutio and Benvolio are discussing wether or not to leave because it is the middle of the day and benvolio does not want to risk a conflict with the capulets when tybalt and his followers enter. mercutio and tybalt argue and romeo enters. tybalt decides he has no quarrel with mercutio and goes to fight romeo. romeo denies Tybalt's challenge and mercutio draws his sword on Tybalt. Tybalt draws on mercutio and they fight. Romeo steps in between the two but Tybalt stabs mercutio under Romeo's arm and runs away. after a little while mercutio dies and Tybalt comes back. Tybalt fights romeo and romeo kills him and leaves. Montage, Capulet and Prince Escales arrives along with some citizens. after some debate between them about what should happen, the Prince decides to exile Romeo.

Scene 2

in this scene, Juliet and Nurse talk about the events in the previous scene. there is some confusion in what Nurse is saying but after a while Juliet learns that Romeo killed Tybalt and Romeo is banished. Juliet asks Nurse to find romeo for her to say farewell.

Scene 3

At the beginning of this scene, Romeo and friar lawrence talk about Romeo's banishment and Nurse arrives. they talk and romeo draws his sword to kill himself but friar lawrence convinces him not to. Nurse tells Romeo that Juliet wants to see him and leaves. Romeo goes to see Juliet.

Scene 4

Capulet talks with Paris about when Paris will marry Juliet. They Decide the wedding will take place on the coming Thursday.

Scene 5

Romeo and Juliet are saying their farewells while Romeo prepares to leave. Nurse enters the chamber to tell Juliet that Lady Capulet is coming and Romeo leaves. Lady Capulet enters and assumes Juliet is weeping over Tybalt when instead it is over Romeo's banishment. They talk about Tybalt's death and Lady Capulet tells Juliet about her marriage to Paris on Thursday. Capulet enters and Juliet says she refuses to marry Paris. Capulet begins to curse his daughter and leaves in a fit of rage, Lady Capulet soon follows. Nurse comforts Juliet and the scene ends.
